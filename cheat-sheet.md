Common "Alphabet" of FlagsSince you are "polishing" your scripts
here are the three most common flags you'll use in DevOps:
FlagMeaningBest for...
-f FileChecking for .current_policy_arn or config.cfg.
-d DirectoryChecking if your logs/ or output/ folder exists.
-s Size (not empty)Checking if a file exists AND actually contains data.
-x ExecutableChecking if you have permission to run a script.
